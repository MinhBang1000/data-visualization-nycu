Visualizing House Property Sales Over Time with a ThemeRiver Chart

Wiggle / Baseline

Feature Guide

  • Hover Interaction: When you hover over a stream, the chart will display a tooltip with information about the property type, the date, and the median price for that stream at that time.
  • Stream Reordering: You can drag and drop the property type legend items on the right to reorder the streams. This allows you to prioritize certain property types and view them at the top of the chart.
  • Toggle Stream Layout: Use the toggle switch at the bottom of the chart to switch between the 'Wiggle' and 'Baseline' stream layouts. The 'Wiggle' layout creates a flowing river-like appearance, while 'Baseline' aligns the streams to a common base for better comparison.
  • Stream Visibility: You can hide or show specific property types by checking or unchecking the checkboxes in the legend. This helps you focus on specific streams without cluttering the chart.
  • Animated Transitions: Whenever you reorder the streams, toggle layouts, or change visibility, the chart will animate these changes with smooth transitions for better clarity.
  • Hover Line: A vertical guide line will appear at the mouse pointer to help you precisely track the position across the streams.
